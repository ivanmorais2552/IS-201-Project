Basketball has always been one of my biggest passions. One of my favorite periods in the sport was the 2020 NBA Bubble. After the COVID-19 pandemic forced the NBA season to pause, the league created a “bubble” at the ESPN Wide World of Sports Complex in Orlando, Florida. Players, coaches, and staff lived and competed in an isolated environment designed to keep everyone safe while allowing the season to continue. Games were played without fans in attendance, creating a unique atmosphere unlike anything the league had experienced before. During that time, I was able to watch almost every game, making the Bubble a memorable experience for me and giving me a deeper appreciation for the sport.
  1. Pure basketball with minimal outside distractions
    • Focus on the game and players' performance
    • Less media hype and off-court drama
  2. No home-court advantage for any team
  3. High level of competition and intensity
    • Teams had to adapt to the unique environment
    • Increased focus on strategy and execution
  4. Unique atmosphere without fans
  5. Opportunity to watch nearly every game during the season restart
